Chicken Alfredo

A rich, creamy italian classic!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breast, cubed
  • 6 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced, divided
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 pound of fettuccini pasta
  • 1/2 onion diced
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cus fresh grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat combine chicken, 2 tablespoons butter, 2 cloves garlic, and Italian seasoning. Cook until chicken is no longer pink inside. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain
  3. Melt 4 tablespoons of butter in a skillet. Saute onion and two cloves of garlic until onions are transparent. Stir in flour, salt and pepper; cook 2 minutes. Slowly add milk, stirring until smooth and creamy. Stir in Parmesan cheese. Stir in chicken mixture and sour cream. Serve over cooked fettuccini.
